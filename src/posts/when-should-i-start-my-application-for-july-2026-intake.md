### Country-Specific Notes:

* **Australia & New Zealand (July Intake - Semester 2):** Applications usually open by late 2025 and close around March-May 2026. Applying early 2026 is key.
* **UK:** For a July/September 2026 start (often called "Autumn" intake), applications typically open in September 2025. UCAS deadlines for most undergrad courses are Jan 2026, but international applications can extend to June 2026. Many postgraduate courses have rolling admissions, so early is best.
* **Canada:** Main intake is September, but some programs have July starts. Deadlines can be as early as December 2025 to February 2026.
* **USA:** Fall (August/September) is the main intake. July 2026 start is less common but possible for some programs. Applications for Fall 2026 usually open August/September 2025, with deadlines often December 2025 - January 2026. Rolling admissions are common, so apply early!

### Why Apply Early?

* **Better Chances:** More spots available.
* **Scholarships:** Many are awarded early.
* **Less Stress:** Ample time for visa processing.
