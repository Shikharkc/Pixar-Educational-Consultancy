Canada is renowned for its high quality of life, excellent education system, and welcoming attitude towards international students. If you're considering Canada for your higher education, understanding the study permit process is key.

## Why Canada?

*   **Quality Education**: Canadian institutions offer a wide range of programs with high academic standards.
*   **Welcoming & Safe**: Known for its multiculturalism and safety.
*   **Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)**: A significant attraction, allowing students to gain Canadian work experience after graduation.
*   **Pathways to Permanent Residency**: Canada offers various routes for international students to apply for permanent residency.

## Study Permit Basics

1.  **Acceptance Letter**: You need an acceptance letter from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
2.  **Proof of Funds**: Demonstrate that you can cover your tuition and living expenses.
3.  **Ties to Home Country**: Show your intent to return home after your studies.
